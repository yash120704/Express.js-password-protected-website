Secrets

  • Always back up your important files in two places: one on the cloud and one on an external drive.
  • Drinking water first thing in the morning can help you feel more energized throughout the day.
  • When learning something new, teach it to someone else—it helps you understand it more deeply.
  • Taking short breaks every 25 minutes while studying or working improves focus and memory (Pomodoro technique).
  • Writing down three things you’re grateful for each day can boost your mood and mindset over time.
  • If you want plants to grow faster, talk to them daily—sound vibrations and carbon dioxide help!
